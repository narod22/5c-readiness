The 5C Framework™

How the 5Cs work.

Think of your business as a road trip.

Where you're going is Context. What you drive is Content. Who's in the car is Community. The rules of the road are Chemistry. The fuel is Commerce.

Miss any one and you're not getting anywhere.

01

Context

Is there market pull?

Roughly 42% of businesses that fail do so because of bad market timing. A great idea in the wrong market still loses. Context tells you whether you're heading somewhere with a tailwind or a headwind.

02

Content

What's your story?

Plenty of businesses make good time. Far fewer know where they're going. Content is the gap between the world as it is today and the world you're trying to build.

03

Community

Who do you serve?

Real community is a world people want to live inside, not an audience that listens to you. Customers, team members, and partners all need a reason to stay.

04

Chemistry

Does it run itself?

If the business only works when you're in the room, it's not a business. It's a job. Chemistry is the people, process, and product that make the thing run without you.

05

Commerce

Does the math work?

Revenue has to grow faster than expenses. Operations have to mirror the model. Miss this and nothing else matters, because there's no fuel in the car.

The Takeaway

The work to make a business replicable is the same work that makes any business run better.

Pass the 5Cs and you're franchise-ready. Don't pass, and you know exactly what to fix. Either way, you end up with a stronger business.
