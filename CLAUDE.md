# 5C Readiness — Ranch Advisors

Public-facing landing page and (eventually) interactive assessment tool for the 5C Franchise Readiness Framework. Top-of-funnel lead generation for Ranch's Franchise Readiness Roadmap consulting engagement.

## What this is

A business owner who's thinking about franchising their concept comes to this page, takes a 50-question self-assessment across Ranch's five pillars (Context, Content, Community, Chemistry, Commerce), and gets a score plus a verdict. Two of the five pillars (Context and Commerce) act as gates: fail either one and no amount of polish elsewhere saves the concept.

Aesthetic reference: owner.com's restraint (interactive tool as the hero, minimal copy, considered layout), rendered in Ranch Advisors' earth-tone brand world. Not a dark SaaS landing page — a light, editorial, operator-grade page with one dark section break for visual rhythm.

## Writing rules

Plain language, no em dashes, no banned words (vibes, fluff, unlock, crush, game-changer, 10x, level up, "everything changed," "nobody talks about"). Short sentences. Connected prose. Copy lives and dies on specificity.
